Elixar Technologies provide services covering a broad spectrum of platforms and technologies including:

  • Digital Marketing
  • Automation Tools and Monitoring Services
These services are packaged in different models and delivered through our agile development process. Our workforce includes dedicated and developed software experts capable of building any complex software as per the client's specifications. Elixar leverages technology and implements the best applications, to provide a range of high quality and cost-efficient solutions, enabling customers to achieve their business goals.

How do you assure confidentiality?

  • We sign contracts and confidentiality agreements as per requirement.
  • We follow confidentiality terms and policies while working, all our employees are bounded by the company confidentiality and non-disclosure clauses.
  • We also have a response plan and employee procedure to keep your project and data safe. Additionally, for sensitive projects, we create isolated teams disconnected from any public network.

Can you guarantee time-bounded results?

Yes. We guarantee fixed-time results without compromising the clarity and delivering optimal results to clients. The completion time will be intimated before signing the deal.

Yes. Search Engine Optimization is offered at an additional cost. Our SEO specialists will help you in choosing the right keywords for your site which will serve you to get top rankings in major search engines.

What modes of payment do you accept?

We accept UPI, Netbanking, IMPS, and all modes of online payment services.

How do I sign-off a contract or work order?

Once the project is complete, we will provide you with a work order form that you have to fill in and sign. As we discuss all the features required, we ourself will send the contract after finalizing the quotation.

What is your TAT?

Our TAT depends on the project and the features required. Normally, it ranges from 15 days to 60 days depending upon the complexity of the project.

How do I associate with Elixar Technologies?

We are just a click away from you. Drop us a mail (contact@elixar.tech) or send a message on the Whatsapp number or the form provided in the contact section, regarding the services you require and details regarding your project and we will contact you shortly.

Obviously, YES. When the project is finished and conveyed to you, we're still bound to deliver you our services in terms of support. We take full responsibility for our work and provide support for bug fixing and minor changes that are part of the project scope with no additional charges.
